Our Story

Pearson Cake Shop is the creation of Kokulan, Sangeethanan, Gopinath, Sanoj and Sivanjah. Pearson Cake shop is started in the year of 2015 and now entering into the 2nd year of anniversary. With a dedicated and friendly staff of over 65 men and women, we strive to provide the best quality food and service always. Comprising of a skilled and artistic staff, we accept orders for wedding structures, design cakes, shape cakes and even customized & personalized sponge creations. Our dynamic catering team is equipped and ready to server any occasion. With the flexibility to provide you with a customized menu, we ensure our service blends smoothly with any event.
